what's actually happening

Communicate → pool → build out the best

Every citizen carries a small idea drawn from its real repo name — its concept tokens. When two drift close they communicate; if their ideas are different enough to be novel yet share enough to cohere, they pool into a hybrid — a new idea with a synthesized name and a one-line pitch. Each hybrid gets a fitness: novel-but-coherent scores highest (the <• rule again — the interesting thing lives in the productive gap between two, not in their sameness or their distance). High-fitness hybrids spawn offspring (the cascade), and the very best are built out into the panel above and kept in the universe's memory.

This is the whole thread, set in motion: imperfect copies (the cross-breed), selection (the cull), the self-describing seed (each idea reseeds), and the gap that does the work — running as one small cosmos.

What's real, and what's the universe dreaming

honest flagThe citizens are your real repositories (names and the concept-tokens parsed from them are facts). Everything the universe generates — the hybrid names, the pitches, the fitness scores, the "communication" — is procedural generation, a working model of idea-evolution, not a claim that any given hybrid is good or that these agents are really thinking. It's a toy cosmos that proposes; you dispose. Treat the built-out ideas as provocations worth a look, not endorsements — some will be nonsense, a few might be real. The value is the process made visible: novelty crossed with coherence, selected over time. Same discipline as the rest — real substrate, honestly-flagged dreaming on top.
